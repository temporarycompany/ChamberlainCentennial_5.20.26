The Approach

Cultural relevance over price point.

The most significant expansion of Chamberlain's audience will not come through institutional channels alone. It will come through the objects, garments, and cultural moments that already structure the daily lives of the people we want to reach.

Each partnership is distributed across 2027 to sustain momentum - reintroducing the work to a new audience with each drop, grounded in a formal or material connection to Chamberlain's practice.

Four Tiers
— Creator. Artists and studios whose practice is in genuine dialogue with Chamberlain's - brought in as collaborators rather than licensees. These partnerships generate the most culturally resonant work and the most durable press.
— Halo. Prestigious, high-visibility moments that anchor the centennial institutionally. These are the drops and events that set the tone and generate sustained conversation - the events everything else orbits.
— Accessible. Broad-reach collaborations that bring the work to new and younger audiences across a range of price points. These create familiarity and establish Chamberlain as a living cultural reference.
— Story. Narrative-first partnerships that prioritize storytelling and cultural context over product. These generate the editorial layer that gives the entire program depth - the "why it matters" that makes the other tiers land.

Q1 — January through March 2027
Setting the tone before the centennial arrives.
Playlab (Creator)  ·  Rimowa (Halo)  ·  Supreme (Accessible)  ·  FAT Racing (Story)
Creator
Artist collaboration. The centennial's cultural proposition.
Playlab
Concept
Playlab serves as creative engine behind the centennial's major activation - an installation, publication, or experiential moment that sets the aesthetic and editorial tone for the entire year. Not just a product; a cultural proposition.
Why It Works
LA-based multidisciplinary studio (Archie Lee Coates IV and Jeff Franklin). Designed Louis Vuitton runway shows for Virgil Abloh, collaborated with Alessi, Rimowa, Nike, and Adidas. Process-driven and ideas-first - exactly right for a centennial moment that should feel unexpected rather than institutional.
Timing
Q1 2027
Launch activation
Halo
Prestige. High-visibility institutional anchor.
Rimowa
Concept
Limited aluminum case edition with a full editorial campaign - material storytelling about worked metal, compression, and the formal language shared between Chamberlain's crushed steel and Rimowa's grooved aluminum shell.
Why It Works
Rimowa's artist collaboration history includes Virgil Abloh, Supreme, Daniel Arsham (eroded suitcase series), Alex Israel, and Dior. LVMH-owned and collector-minded. Arsham's Rimowa work - including an "eroded suitcase" numbered series - establishes a direct precedent for sculptural object thinking applied to travel goods.
Timing
Q1 2027
Editorial and retail launch
Accessible
Broad reach. New audiences. Multiple price points.
Supreme
Concept
Centennial capsule - tee, hoodie, and skate deck featuring archival Chamberlain imagery. Limited run at Supreme standalone stores and DSM shop-in-shop, driving heat on the secondary market.
Why It Works
The defining institution of artist-apparel collaboration. Prior programs with Basquiat and Haring estates prove Supreme treats legacy partnerships with rigor. Their collector audience generates secondary market momentum that sustains cultural conversation long after the drop.
Timing
Q1 2027
Ahead of centennial
Story
Narrative first. Cultural context. Event-driven.
FAT Racing
Concept
A centennial activation at or around FAT International's GP Ice Race - a Chamberlain-themed presence at one of automotive culture's most culturally sophisticated events. More culturally resonant than the St. Moritz Ice Race circuit; FAT's audience is younger, more design-literate, and deeply community-driven.
Why It Works
FAT International was founded by Ferdinand "Ferdi" Porsche (grandson of the company founder). The GP Ice Race brings together fashion, art, music, and motorsport in a way few events do. Chamberlain's automotive steel origins make this a genuinely earned presence - not a brand placement but a natural cultural alignment between two practices rooted in the material culture of the car.
Timing
Feb / Mar 2027
GP Ice Race season

Q2 — April through June 2027 ★ Centennial
April 16, 2027. The centennial itself.
Sterling Ruby (Creator)  ·  Alessi (Halo)  ·  424 (Accessible)  ·  VCARB / Racing Bulls F1 (Story)
Creator
Artist reinterpretation. The centennial's most formally resonant collaboration.
Sterling Ruby
S.R. STUDIO. LA. CA. — Los Angeles
Concept
A centennial collaboration in which Ruby reinterprets Chamberlain's formal and material language through his own practice - sculpture, textile, ceramics, or painting - creating new work in genuine dialogue with Chamberlain's output rather than simply licensing it.
Why It Works
Ruby's monumental metal sculptures recall industrial machine parts; his "illicit mergers" of disparate materials directly echo Chamberlain's formal logic. Both engage American material culture at scale. His clothing line S.R. STUDIO. LA. CA. (debuted Pitti Uomo 2019) gives this Creator collaboration natural product extensions.
Timing
April 2027
Centennial week
Halo
Prestige object. The centennial's defining design artifact.
Alessi
Concept
Limited artist object series - steel and chrome home objects designed in dialogue with Chamberlain's formal language of crumple, compression, and unexpected beauty. Chamberlain's painted steel masks are a natural application for Alessi's corkscrew and tabletop object language. Edition of 999.
Why It Works
Alessi has produced limited-edition artist objects since the 1970s - from Ettore Sottsass to Virgil Abloh (edition sold through instantly, now in MoMA's permanent collection). Their design-literate collector audience is precisely the audience for whom the centennial should carry weight. The material overlap is direct: both practices center highly finished metal objects that are simultaneously functional and contemplative.
Timing
April 2027
Centennial week
Accessible
LA streetwear credibility. Fine art meets fashion.
424
Guillermo Andrade — Los Angeles, Est. 2015
Concept
A centennial capsule grounded in Chamberlain's material practice - hand-finished garments in steel-toned palettes with abstract painted details referencing the sculpture's surface and process. A collaboration that treats apparel as an extension of the artist's formal language, not merely a graphic application.
Why It Works
Guillermo Andrade's 424 sits at the intersection of fine art and streetwear - sold at SSENSE, Selfridges, and GR8 Tokyo. His collections have tackled police brutality, immigration, and American identity: the same territory Chamberlain's practice occupies. 424 is deeply rooted in Los Angeles and a specifically American material and cultural experience. This is the most conceptually coherent apparel collaboration in the program.
Timing
April / May 2027
Miami Art Week / centennial
Story

VCARB / Racing Bulls
Content Program
Concept
The F1 partnership extends beyond the livery into a full content program - the story of American steel from Chamberlain's studio to the F1 pit lane. Short-form video, archival imagery, and editorial distributed through VCARB's channels and the estate's platforms simultaneously.
Why It Works
VCARB's Creator Platform provides built-in distribution infrastructure reaching F1's global digital audience - young, design-literate, and culturally curious. The content turns the livery from a one-day visual into a weeks-long conversation that far outlasts the race weekend, generating organic reach across platforms the estate cannot access alone.

Q3 — July through September 2027
Art world re-entry. The summer sustained.
Samuel Ross / SR_A (Creator)  ·  Tiffany & Co. (Halo)  ·  Hidden NY (Accessible)  ·  Cart Dept. (Story)
Creator
Fine art credential. Design world authority.
Samuel Ross
SR_A Studio, London
Concept
A reinterpretation of Chamberlain's formal language through SR_A's brutalist industrial design vocabulary - a sculptural object, furniture edition, or spatial installation that processes Chamberlain's material logic through Ross's own idiom.
Why It Works
Samuel Ross MBE founded SR_A after A-COLD-WALL*. Partners: Apple, LVMH, Kohler, Zara. Works held at The Met, V&A, and The Design Museum. First Virgil Abloh design assistant. His practice - abstraction, brutalism, deconstruction, "social architecture for the body" - is in close formal conversation with Chamberlain's compression and material ambition. Artistic Director of the 2025 London Design Biennale.
Timing
Q3 2027
Summer / art fair season
Halo
Luxury jewelry. Institutional fine art credibility.
Tiffany & Co.
Concept
A centennial silver jewelry edition - sculptural sterling pieces referencing the compressed, torqued geometry of Chamberlain's steel works. Tiffany's history in architectural and sculptural jewelry makes this a coherent and prestigious extension of the centennial program.
Why It Works
Tiffany's recent cultural collaborations - including a Tiffany x Rimowa case (2023) and sustained LVMH-era cultural programming - demonstrate a genuine appetite for institutional partnerships that carry fine art credibility. A Chamberlain centennial silver edition positions the house within a serious art world narrative at the highest tier of the jewelry market.
Timing
September 2027
Accessible
Discovery engine. Next-gen tastemaker.
Hidden NY
Concept
A web-drop centennial capsule with accompanying editorial - not just a product but a documented encounter with Chamberlain's work. Storytelling-first, limited run, community-focused. Hidden NY's format means the collaboration arrives with genuine narrative depth, not just a logo.
Why It Works
Hidden NY operates as a discovery and education engine for the next generation of culturally voracious consumers - the audience that finds things before anyone else. Their platforms function as the tastemaker layer: the place a younger collector encounters something before it becomes mainstream. A Chamberlain centennial drop here is an introduction, not a reminder.
Timing

Cart Dept.
Concept
An archival storytelling partnership - Cart Dept. as the editorial and discovery engine that introduces Chamberlain's practice to an audience that finds things before anyone else. Deep-dive content, archival presentation, and centennial context delivered in the register next-gen collectors actually read.

Cart Dept. is a culturally credible archive and discovery platform built for the digitally native collector. This is the editorial layer that makes the other drops make sense.

Q4 — October through December 2027
Collector season. The year's final argument.
Bentley (Creator)  ·  Hot Wheels / Mattel Creations (Halo)  ·  Uniqlo UT (Accessible)  ·  Design Miami (Story)
Creator
The ultimate automotive collaboration. Craft meets legacy.
Bentley
Concept
A limited centennial commission through Bentley Mulliner - a bespoke vehicle finished in a custom colorway and material referencing Chamberlain's steel palette, paired with a sculptural object or artwork created for the partnership. Salon Prive as the reveal context.
Why It Works
Bentley Mulliner's bespoke program has the craft vocabulary to engage with Chamberlain's formal language at the highest tier of automotive culture. Their collector clientele overlaps precisely with the serious fine art audience. Bentley has previously commissioned artist collaborations that treat the car as a cultural object - not simply a vehicle. The partnership closes the centennial with a genuinely prestigious automotive statement that honors the material origins of Chamberlain's practice.
Timing
Q4 2027
Salon Prive
Halo
The collector object. Secondary market currency.
Hot Wheels
Mattel Creations
Concept
Limited edition centennial collector series - 1:64 die-cast vehicles referencing Chamberlain's crushed-steel aesthetic in premium display packaging. A holiday release positions this as the centennial's definitive collectible object - affordable, desirable, and unmistakably connected to Chamberlain's material origins.
Why It Works
Chamberlain's practice originated in crushed automotive steel - Hot Wheels are, literally, tiny versions of his material. The conceptual link is direct and requires no translation. Mattel Creations' Red Line Club and Elite 64 editions trade at multiples of retail on the secondary market. Their existing Daniel Arsham collaboration (the Livery Porsche 930A) provides a direct precedent and potential bridge to this program.
Timing
Q4 2027
Holiday release
Accessible
Global reach. Maximum distribution. Every price point.
Uniqlo UT
Concept
UT graphic tee collection translating Chamberlain's steel surface photography and color fields into printable form. Global distribution across 2,400+ stores worldwide - the most democratic and scalable centennial touchpoint in the entire program.
Why It Works
Uniqlo's UT program has worked with artists from Andy Warhol to Louis de Guzman. Accessible price point removes every barrier to entry. Global physical distribution means Chamberlain's centennial reaches places that no gallery show, editorial, or digital campaign can replicate. This is the program's widest net - timed to holiday season for maximum reach.
Timing
Q4 2027
Holiday season global launch
Story
Design world capstone. The centennial's final chapter.
Design Miami
Concept
A year-end cultural capstone - Chamberlain centennial presence at Design Miami, the definitive annual meeting point of design, art, and collecting. A presentation, partnership, or installation that closes the centennial year with institutional design world authority and collector-facing gravitas.
Why It Works
Design Miami draws the most concentrated gathering of serious design collectors, curators, and institutions in the world each December. A centennial presence here - timed to the program's final major release - positions the year's work within the permanent record of design history rather than the ephemeral cycle of cultural marketing. The right place to land the centennial's final argument.
Timing
December 2027
